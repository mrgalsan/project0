Park attraction parameters:

  • availability from home within less than one hour by car, so we shrink in my case with NJ parks only,northern and central part;
  • having parking slots in satisfactional amount considering that the same search is performed by plenty of seekers who wanted to have a rest from being locked in apartaments;
  • having something interesting for sight pleasure;
  • having convinient walkways, at least wide to keep up social distance;
  • any food distribution facilities in close avalability;
  • spots to sit or place yourself without blocking paths and walkways;
  • nice to have acess to the river, pond, lake, ocean, waterfall;
  • clean and maintained facilities;
  • if the ticket is required and what is your expenses;
  • If the guards are presented and how do they behave;
